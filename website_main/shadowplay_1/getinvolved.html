Welcome To The Creekside Community Co-op

How To Get Involved With us:

We will be hosting an "open house" where you can come visit us during the month of may we have many oppurtunites for you an your family to come visit and see how we run our classes and meet new friends. If you get interested in joining us you will get a packet of information to fill out so your child or children can enroll the following year!

Church Requirements:

Because this Co-op is using the creekside communtiy church we use up quite a bit of their toilet paper, paper towels, Etc. Rquirements for Joining: - 1 roll of paper towels - 1 package of toilet paper (about nine rolls) - 1 ream of paper (these donations occur in the middle of the year and towards the end.)

When you agree to be a part of our Co-op community ech family is required to teach two classes, you also must bring your own lunch it will not be provided for you the kitchen at he church is free to use. There is some availible supplies at the co-op, however most items needed for classes will either need to be purchased by you or ask the parents of your students to but them.
